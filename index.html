About the Summit

The Global Education Summit brings together world leaders, policymakers, and education advocates in a unified mission to address the critical issue of out-of-school children. In an era where education is the cornerstone of development, we're committed to breaking down barriers that prevent millions of children from accessing quality learning opportunities.

Through collaborative dialogue, innovative solutions, and actionable frameworks, we're building bridges to education for every child, everywhere.
